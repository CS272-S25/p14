A more personal side of who we are! Hear our mission, story, and what makes us different from other productivity sites.

Mission Statement

We believe studying should be structured, motivating, and enjoyable. That’s why we created a productivity tracker designed for students who want to stay focused, organized, and on top of their goals.

Our Story

When approached with the project guidelines, we wanted to create something meaningful and personally useful. As students ourselves, we constantly juggle tracking assignments, maintaining study streaks, and staying motivated. We realized the need for a central platform that blends organization with motivation—so we built one.

  • A to-do list of assignments organized by urgency and importance
  • A goals section broken into short-term and long-term milestones
  • A built-in Pomodoro timer to optimize focus
  • A calendar overview to visualize deadlines
  • And a few exciting features coming soon...
